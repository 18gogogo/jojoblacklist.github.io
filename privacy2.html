PRIVACY POLICY

This document outlines the responsibilities and policies of “18Deal” pursuant to the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486 of the Laws of Hong Kong) (“the Ordinance”).
Users may be required to provide personal information to access certain features, services, or facilities offered through the Website. Under the Ordinance, “18Deal” undertakes to maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and security of all personal data collected. Such data may include, but is not limited to, email addresses and other information voluntarily provided through the Website.
To fulfill operational and service delivery requirements, “18Deal” may disclose personal data to the following parties:

* Affiliated companies and joint ventures of “18Deal”;
* Agents, contractors, or service providers engaged in providing services related to “18Deal’s” business operations;
* Media outlets, where necessary in handling customer complaints or inquiries;
* Any person to whom disclosure is required by law or regulatory obligation;
* Any other party bound by a duty of confidentiality to “18Deal”.

The Website is designed to be suitable for general public and family use. However, individuals under the age of eighteen (18) should not submit any personal data unless authorized by a parent or legal guardian.
Under the Ordinance, data subjects have the following rights:

* To request confirmation as to whether “18Deal” holds personal data relating to them;
* To obtain a copy of such data;
* To request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Requests for access, review, or correction of personal data must be submitted in writing and sent to:
admin@18gogogo.top
A reasonable administrative fee of HK$800 will be charged to cover the costs associated with processing such requests.
If you do not wish to receive promotional materials (excluding communications directly related to “18Deal”), please log into your Member Center, navigate to "Update Membership Details," and uncheck the box labeled “Receive Member Messages (Email).” Upon receipt of your request, “18Deal” will promptly cease using your personal data for direct marketing purposes at no cost.
All personal data collected by “18Deal” is used exclusively for purposes directly related to the provision and distribution of “18Deal” services, including membership management, service delivery, and communication. To receive such services, users must provide the necessary personal information. For further details regarding our Privacy Policy, please refer to this page.

We implement robust electronic, physical, and procedural safeguards to protect personal data in compliance with the standards prescribed under the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance.

“18Deal” reserves the right to update or revise this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Should there be significant changes to how personal data is collected, used, or disclosed, we will post a prominent notice on the Website to inform users of such revisions.
