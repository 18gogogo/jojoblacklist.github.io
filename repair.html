3 Essential Renovation Tips

Buying a property is exciting, but renovation projects can turn into nightmares if not handled properly. Follow these professional tips to protect your investment and maintain peace of mind.

Tip 1: Verify Custom Furniture Specifications

Real Case: A homeowner discovered bathroom doors didn't match ordered specifications post-renovation, leading to disputes and damaged relationships.

Professional Advice:

  • Confirm manufacturer capabilities with your contractor
  • Obtain detailed measurements before ordering
  • Verify materials, dimensions, and colors before installation

Tip 2: Define Post-Completion Fixes

Key Considerations:

  • Multiple trades (plumbing, electrical, etc.) create complex dependencies
  • Minor imperfections are inevitable
  • Contract must include snagging clause with:
    • Clear responsibility matrix
    • Timeline for fixes
    • Quality standards

Tip 3: Structured Payment Schedule

Payment Protocol:

  • Typical project cost: HKD 100k-500k
  • Split payments into 3-4 milestones
  • Final payment (10-15%) after:
    • Complete snagging fixes
    • Final inspection approval

⚠️ Warning: Never pay full amount upfront! Maintain financial leverage for negotiations.

Pro Tips for Success

  • Conduct bi-weekly site inspections
  • Maintain open communication with contractors
  • Document all changes and agreements
